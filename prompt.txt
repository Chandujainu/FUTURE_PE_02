I wanted to create social media marketing content for a beauty salon using AI.

I asked ChatGPT to generate five User-Generated Content (UGC) advertisement scripts for "Radiance Beauty Salon."

Each advertisement should include:
- A catchy hook
- Customer problem
- Personal experience
- Solution
- Benefits
- Call-to-Action (CTA)
- Social media caption
- Relevant hashtags

The content should sound natural, authentic, and engaging, as if a real customer is sharing their experience. It should be suitable for Instagram Reels, Facebook, YouTube Shorts, and TikTok.
